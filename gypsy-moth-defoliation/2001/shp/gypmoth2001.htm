What does this data set describe?

Title: gypmoth2001
Abstract:
This dataset includes polygons of tree defoliation caused by gypsy moth caterpillars. They are delimited annually on 1:100,000 scale maps by aerial observers in small aircraft from altitudes of 1000 to 1500 feet above ground level. Data for this layer were collected summer of 2001.
Supplemental_Information:
All defoliation mapped during this survey was moderate to heavy.

Why was the data set created?
