Description:
Abstract:
Forest landuse for Louisa County as defined by the U.S. Forest Service Field Instructions for Southern Forest Inventory landuse classification definition of forest. According to this definition, the minimum size requirement for a forest area is at least 1 acre and 120 ft. in width a the point of occurence. Using this rule set, the forest landuse was digitized from available USGS DOQQ Imagery, 1:12000 scale.
Purpose:
The forest landuse was digitized as part of the Louisa County Forest Sustainability project to assess the geographic distribution of forests and their relationship to other features, such as property ownership parcels.
Supplemental_Information:
Using the landuse classification rules described in the USFS FIA Handbook, the forest landuse was digitized from available USGS DOQQ Imagery, 1:12000 scale. These data only contain forested landuse areas 1 acre or greater. It does not contain other land use classifcations, such as water, agriculture,urban areas or barren/stip land. There are currently no plans to perform this kind of delineation for additional counties in the Commonwealth

Completeness_Report:
The data were derived using the rules for delineating forest landuse parcels described in the USFS Field Instructions for Southern Forest Inventory. Landuse other than forest was not delineated. The forest landuse delineation was based on 1994 Digital Orthophoto Quarter Quad (USGS) imagery and does not reflect any differences in forest landuse predating this time, nor changes since that time.
